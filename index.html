Welcome





The Cairo Housing Authority has one objective and this objective is to provide safe and sanitary housing for you and your family.


Located in Cairo Nebraska, the Hulett Park Apartments provide us an opportunity to serve your housing needs in an affordable and nondiscriminitory manner.

Hulett Park Apartments offers many individuals the opportunity for independent living at an affordable price.


Our office is open from 5:30-9:30 pm Monday and Friday.
We also accept pre-arranged appointments.

CHA does not discriminate on the basis of handicap, race, color, religion, sex, familial status or national origin in the admission or access to, or treatment or employment in its federally assisted programs and activities.
